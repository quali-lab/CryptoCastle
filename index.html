OVER 5 THOUSAND YEARS OF HISTORY AND INNOVATION

Yesterday, today, and tomorrow

The Castle of Burgscheidungen is an unparalleled example of Baroque decadence in the region and Germany. With an archaeological history dating back to 3200 BCE, it features a unique combination of baroque and renaissance styles, and holds the honour of being one of the oldest and best-preserved sites in all of Europe.
